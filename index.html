Who We Are ?

Tec is a resource center / organization which is working to support teachers, Students and School management team equally in all domains where the support is required.

Education is ever learning process and we are still learning, our motto is to strive for academic excellence in the field of education so, Tec team is working hard to cater all the needs or requirements of our education system.

The main purpose of Tec to provide suitable and reliable solution under one roof of all kinds for educational organizations or students.
